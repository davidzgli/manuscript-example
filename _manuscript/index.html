Abstract

Using some common datasets, we demonstrate the use of Quarto to create a manuscript with multiple sections and figures. The manuscript includes an overview of the mtcars dataset, plots of the mtcars dataset, an overview of the iris dataset, and plots of the iris dataset.

3.2 mtcars plots

Fig. 1 shows the boxplot of disp in the mtcars dataset for different gears. Fig 2 shows the scatter plot between mpg and disp in the mtcars dataset.

Fig. 3 shows the combined plot of a colored scatter and a bar plot for the mtcars dataset. Fig. 3 A indicates weight has positive correlation with hp, with higher weights having larger horsepower, accompanied by lower mpg. We can see that four forward gears are most popular for cars with 4 or 6 cylinders, while 3 gears are most popular for cars with 8 cylinders (Fig. 3 B).

3.4 Iris plots

Supplementary Fig.1 shows the scatter plot of sepal length and sepal width for the iris dataset. Supplementary Fig.2 shows the scatter plot of petal length and petal width for the iris dataset.
